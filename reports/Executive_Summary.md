# Executive Summary

## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition patterns using Python, SQL, and Tableau to identify workforce trends, attrition drivers, employee demographics, and job satisfaction insights.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of employee retention metrics and supports data-driven HR decision-making.

---

## Key Metrics

- Total Employees: 1,470
- Attrition Count: 237
- Attrition Rate: 16.12%
- Active Employees: 1,233
- Average Employee Age: 37 Years

---

## Dashboard Highlights

- Department-wise attrition analysis
- Employee age distribution analysis
- Job satisfaction heatmap by role
- Education field attrition analysis
- Gender and age-group attrition comparison

---

## Business Value

The dashboard helps HR teams identify high-risk employee segments, understand workforce demographics, monitor satisfaction levels, and develop retention strategies to reduce employee turnover.
